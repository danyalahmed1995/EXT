# Navigation in EXT

EXT is designed to keep you focused on your work while making file traversal incredibly fast and intuitive.

## The Sidebar
The sidebar on the left is your primary navigation hub. 
- **Smart Views**: Quickly filter your active workspace by recency, favorites, or specific file types like `.md` and `.txt`.
- **Workspaces**: Switch between different project folders seamlessly.

## Quick File Search
Tired of clicking through folders? Use the **Search bar** at the top of the sidebar. It instantly filters all files within your active workspace by name, letting you jump to the exact document you need in milliseconds.

## Split View
Need to reference another file while writing? 
1. Open a file.
2. Click the **Split** button at the top right of the editor.
3. You can now view two files side-by-side or preview the current file's rendered output next to the raw markdown!

## Focus Mode
Want to remove all distractions? The sidebar automatically recedes into a sleek, minimal view when you hover away, letting the editor take over the entire screen. Your thoughts stay front and center.
